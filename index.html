Postdoc at Münster

Until May 2024, I was postdoc at the Institute for Analysis and Numerics at the University of Münster. I was supervised by Prof. Dr. M. Pirner. My research position was embedded into the DFG project number 456849348 (Modeling and mathematical description of concrete physical applications in the context of kinetic theory using the Bathnagar-Gross-Krook equation), see Research » Projects.
