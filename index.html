Dr. Dennis Ulbrich

Postdoctoral researcher

Until May 2024, I was postdoc at the Institute for Analysis and Numerics at the University of Münster. I was supervised by Prof. Dr. M. Pirner. My research position was embedded into the DFG project number 456849348 (Modeling and mathematical description of concrete physical applications in the context of kinetic theory using the Bathnagar-Gross-Krook equation), see Research.

Beforehand, I did my PhD (Dr. rer. nat. in Mathematics) at the University of Bremen, also see MGP. My advisors were Prof. Dr. J. Rademacher and Prof. Dr. M. Keßeböhmer. I was supported by the DFG project number 384027439 (Ergodic theory of nonlinear waves in discrete and continuous excitable media), also see Research.

I also worked as a lecturer and in the IT sector, see Other.
