About

I am a postdoctoral mathematician with experience in research and teaching, student supervision and academic course organization.

Research interests

  • Ordinary and partial differential equations and their dynamics, in particular nonlinear waves
  • Kinetic theory, in particular entropy methods
  • Boltzmann equation and its BGK-type approximations
  • Long-time behavior of solutions (hypocoercivity)
  • Physical applications
  • Models of chemical reactions
