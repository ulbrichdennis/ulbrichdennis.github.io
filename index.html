Research Statement

My research focuses on nonlinear evolution equations with an emphasis on dynamical systems, kinetic theory, and their interplay. I am particularly interested in qualitative properties such as long-time behavior, stability, and ergodic aspects of solutions, as well as connections between analytical theory and applications.
