Research Statement

My research focuses on nonlinear evolution equations with an emphasis on dynamical systems, kinetic theory, and their interplay. I am particularly interested in qualitative properties such as long-time behavior, stability, and ergodic aspects of solutions.

Teaching & Teaching Profile

I am strongly committed to high-quality undergraduate teaching and to long-term teaching-oriented academic roles (e.g. Lecturer / Lehrkraft für besondere Aufgaben). My teaching combines mathematical rigor with accessibility and aims to support diverse student backgrounds in both large introductory courses and more advanced formats.
