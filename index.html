Dr. Dennis Ulbrich

Mathematician · Nonlinear Analysis · Dynamical Systems

About. Mathematician with research focus in nonlinear analysis, dynamical systems, ergodic theory and kinetic theory; several years of teaching experience in undergraduate mathematics, student supervision, and academic course organization

Research interests

  • Ordinary and partial differential equations and their dynamics
  • Nonlinear wave solutions and their ergodic properties
  • Kinetic theory, in particular entropy methods
  • Boltzmann equation and its BGK-type approximations
  • Long-time behavior of solutions (hypocoercivity)
  • Physical applications
  • Models of chemical reactions
