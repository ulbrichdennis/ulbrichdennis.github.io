About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute for Analysis and Numerics of the University of Münster.

Beforehand, I did my PhD (Dr. rer. nat. in Mathematics) at the University of Bremen; further information: CV.

Research interests

  • Dynamics of ordinary and partial differential equations, dynamical systems, ergodic theory
  • Kinetic theory, kinetic equations, long-time behaviour of solutions
  • Boltzmann equation and BGK-type approximations
  • Applications

Current project

  • Modeling and mathematical description of concrete physical applications in the context of kinetic theory using the Bathnagar-Gross-Krook equation

    (DFG project 456849348, supervised by Prof. M. Pirner)
