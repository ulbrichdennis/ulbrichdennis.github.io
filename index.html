Dr. Dennis Ulbrich

Mathematician with research background in nonlinear analysis, dynamical systems, ergodic theory, and kinetic theory. Strong commitment to undergraduate teaching and academic course organization.

Research Interests

  • Ordinary and partial differential equations
  • Nonlinear wave solutions and ergodic properties
  • Kinetic theory and entropy methods
  • Boltzmann equation and BGK models
  • Long-time behavior (hypocoercivity)
  • Applications in physics and chemistry
