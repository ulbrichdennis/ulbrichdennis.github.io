About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute for Analysis and Numerics of the University of Münster supervised by Prof. M. Pirner.

Beforehand, I did my PhD (Dr. rer. nat. in Mathematics) at the University of Bremen (supervisors: Prof. J. Rademacher, Prof. M. Keßeböhmer).

Research interests

  • Dynamics of ordinary and partial differential equations, dynamical systems, ergodic theory
  • Kinetic theory, kinetic equations, long-time behaviour of solutions
  • Boltzmann equation and BGK-type approximations
  • Applications
