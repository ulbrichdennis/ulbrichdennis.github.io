This module was the second semester in a sequence of mathematical methods modules for students in the sciences, industrial engineering, and management majors. It aimed at rounding off the mathematical education for students in these majors with topics from matrix algebra, probability, and related subjects in a way that is directly useful for the applications in experimental sciences, economics, management, and applied engineering.
